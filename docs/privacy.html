Privacy Policy

YouTube Activity Cleaner

Last updated: April 10, 2026

YouTube Activity Cleaner is designed to run locally in your browser. The extension helps you automate repetitive delete actions on the Google My Activity comments page for your own signed-in Google account.

What Data The Extension Accesses

  • The extension reads and interacts with the Google My Activity comments page at https://myactivity.google.com/* so it can find visible delete controls and status messages.
  • The extension stores your cleaner preferences locally in Chrome, such as pacing, retry count, and failure limits.
  • The extension uses the Chrome tabs and power APIs to open the comments page, track the active cleaner tab, and request keep-awake while a run is active.

What Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share your browsing history, comment content, account credentials, or personal data with external servers.
  • We do not send your data to analytics, ads, or third-party tracking systems.
  • We do not use remote code execution or fetch external scripts to operate the extension.

Where Data Is Stored

Cleaner settings are stored locally in Chrome storage on your device. Session information about the currently attached cleaner tab may also be stored in browser session storage so the popup can reconnect to the current run.
